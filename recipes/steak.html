Steak

This is beef of the cow cooked utilizing heat from the sun

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Meat
  • Heat
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Raise cow to butcher for its meat
  2. Wait for a day where it will be warm and sunny
  3. Gather foil to create a sun oven
  4. Put meat into sun oven at dawn, lather meat in butter and salt
  5. Apply pepper at noon with more salt
  6. When the sun has set the steak is cooked, take the meat form the oven and plate it
  7. Garnish with ketchup
