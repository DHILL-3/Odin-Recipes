I have gathered you here today to help you make the greatest breakfast item on the menu. Cereal!

What you need

  • Oats
  • Salt
  • H2O
  • Cinnamon

What you need to do

  1. Measure out oats and water
  2. THROW oats into water
  3. Add three tablespoons of salt
  4. Heat in microwave for 5 minutes and 35 seconds, stirring halfway through
  5. Add 1 ounce of cinnamon
  6. Bon appetit!
